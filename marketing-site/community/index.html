Community Guidelines

Effective Date: March 22, 2026

Our community thrives on respect, creativity, and shared passion for Bucharest's nightlife. These guidelines help ensure a safe, welcoming, and enjoyable experience for everyone.

Our Core Values

We believe in:

  • Respect: Treating everyone with dignity and kindness
  • Inclusivity: Welcoming people of all backgrounds, identities, and experiences
  • Authenticity: Encouraging genuine self-expression and sharing
  • Safety: Prioritizing the well-being of all community members
  • Fun: Celebrating the joy and excitement of nightlife culture

Community Guidelines

🤝 Be Respectful and Kind

Treat others as you'd like to be treated. Harassment, hate speech, bullying, or discriminatory content of any kind is strictly prohibited.

📝 Keep It Authentic

Share genuine experiences and content. Misleading information, fake accounts, or impersonation is not allowed.

🔒 Respect Privacy

Don't share personal information about others without their consent. Respect boundaries and confidentiality.

📸 Share Responsibly

When posting about venues or events, ensure your content is accurate and respectful. Don't promote illegal activities or unsafe behavior.

🚫 No Spam or Self-Promotion

Unsolicited promotion, advertising, or spam is prohibited. This includes repetitive content, unwanted messages, or commercial solicitation.

⚖️ Follow the Law

Don't use our platform to facilitate or promote illegal activities, including drug use, underage drinking, or any criminal behavior.

🎵 Respect Copyright

Only share content you have the right to share. Respect intellectual property rights of artists, photographers, and creators.

Enforcement

We take these guidelines seriously to protect our community. Violations may result in:

  • Content removal
  • Warning notifications
  • Temporary account restrictions
  • Permanent account bans for severe or repeated violations

We rely on community reporting to help identify guideline violations. If you see something concerning, please use the reporting feature within the app or contact us directly.
